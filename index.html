About Me

Nafisa Soeltan works as an IT Consultant/Business Analyst within the Life Sciences Industry.

She has an MBA in Management of Information Systems and a BA in Psychology from Rowan University in New Jersey. She also has a BS Certificate in Business Economics from Inholland University in the Netherlands.

Currently, she is enrolled in Penn's LPS 2018 Coding Bootcamp to increase her technical knowledge of IT systems and challenge her creativity.

She loves traveling around the world and exploring new cultures. Her most exciting experience was riding a smelly camel on a beach and scuba diving in Egypt. The most breathtaking beaches she has been to are all located in Greece.
